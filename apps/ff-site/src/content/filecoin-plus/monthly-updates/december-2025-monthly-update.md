## Year in Review

2025 was a landmark year for the Filecoin Plus program. Here are the key highlights.

## Key Achievements

- Total DataCap distributed exceeded 2 EiB
- Allocator count grew from 30 to 48
- New compliance framework launched
- Improved audit tooling deployed

## Metrics Summary

| Metric              | Q1      | Q2      | Q3      | Q4      |
| ------------------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| DataCap Distributed | 400 PiB | 500 PiB | 550 PiB | 600 PiB |
| New Allocators      | 5       | 4       | 6       | 3       |

## Looking Ahead to 2026

The program will focus on scaling allocator operations and improving the governance process for DataCap distribution.
