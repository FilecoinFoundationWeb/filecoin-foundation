Spring 2026 has been one of the most active seasons in Filecoin's history. From new storage provider onboarding records to community-led hackathons across three continents, the ecosystem continues to grow in both scale and diversity.

## Storage Network Milestones

The Filecoin network crossed a major threshold this quarter, with total storage capacity surpassing 25 exbibytes. This growth was driven in part by a wave of new storage providers in Southeast Asia and Latin America, regions that have seen increased interest in decentralized infrastructure.

## Community Events

The Filecoin community organized over 40 meetups worldwide during March and April alone. Highlights included a storage provider workshop in Seoul, a developer bootcamp in Nairobi, and the annual Filecoin Day at ETH Denver, which drew over 500 attendees.

## Looking Ahead

With FIL Dev Summit 9 on the horizon and several major protocol upgrades in testing, the second half of 2026 promises to be even more eventful. Stay tuned for announcements about community grants, upcoming hackathons, and new ecosystem partnerships.
