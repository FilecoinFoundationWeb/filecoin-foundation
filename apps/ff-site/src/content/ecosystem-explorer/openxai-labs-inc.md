We are OpenxAI Labs Inc, a New York based open source AI infrastructure project with a community of over 20,000 users. 

OpenxAI is building a permissionless AI infrastructure and monetization platform where developers can build, deploy, and commercialize AI applications, agents, models, datasets, and workflows across cloud, hybrid, and local environments.

We use Filecoin and IPFS as part of our decentralized storage layer for AI related assets and application infrastructure. This includes storing model artifacts, datasets, metadata, application files, agent configurations, user generated AI outputs, marketplace records, and provenance related information in a more open, verifiable, and resilient way.

Filecoin fits our mission because AI should not depend only on centralized storage providers, closed SaaS platforms, or traditional cloud infrastructure. Developers need a way to store and access AI assets globally while maintaining ownership, portability, and long term availability.

In OpenxAI v10, our goal is to make Filecoin and IPFS accessible to developers through simple templates and deployment flows. Builders should be able to launch an AI app, connect storage, attach monetization, and publish their service without needing a bank account, credit card processor, or centralized platform dependency.

We see Filecoin as an important infrastructure layer for decentralized AI because it can support model storage, dataset storage, app metadata, AI agent memory archives, marketplace provenance, and long term records for tokenized AI assets.

OpenxAI’s broader mission is to help developers around the world launch AI companies and AI services from a wallet, with decentralized compute, decentralized storage, and onchain monetization working together as one platform.
